# Product Definition

## Sesari: Autonomous B2B SaaS Growth Agent

Sesari is an autonomous B2B SaaS growth agent designed to bridge the "Execution Gap" by connecting high-level growth ambitions to live business signals from integrated platforms like Stripe and HubSpot.

## Target Users

- Lean SaaS founders
- Growth leads at B2B SaaS companies

## Core Value Proposition

Sesari eliminates the gap between strategic growth goals and tactical execution by continuously monitoring business signals and autonomously taking action to drive growth outcomes.
